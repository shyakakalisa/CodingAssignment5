This recipe is perfect for any occasion. Follow these steps to cook a flavorful dish that everyone will love.

Rating

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Measurement Conversion

1 cup = 240 ml

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • Baking tray

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and eggs in the bowl.
  3. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
